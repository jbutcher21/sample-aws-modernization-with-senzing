If you are joining an AWS-hosted event, you will use a platform named *AWS Workshop Studio*. This platform allows AWS field teams to run Workshops, Game Days, Boot camps, Immersion Days, and other events that require hands-on access to AWS accounts or resources.

Follow the steps below to gain access to your Workshop Studio AWS account:

#### Step 1: Go to Workshop Studio

Access your workshop here: [Workshop Studio](https://catalog.workshops.aws/join)

#### Step 2:. Choose Sign-In method.

<p style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Sign In dialog" src="/images/aws_event/sign_in.png">
</p>

- Select "**Email one-time-password (OTP)**" for AWS guided events.

#### Step 3: Enter your Email

<p style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Email Setup" src="/images/aws_event/enter_email.png">
</p>

- Enter your email and click "**Send Passcode**".

#### Step 4: Enter OTP

<p style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Email OTP" src="/images/aws_event/enter_otp.png">
</p>

- Check your email for a message titled "Verify your AWS Training and Certification email address".
- Copy the passcode and enter it here.
- Click **Sign in**.

#### Step 5: Enter Event Access Code (If prompted)

<p style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Event access code" src="/images/aws_event/event_access_code.png">
</p>

- Paste the 12-character code from your organizer and click "**Next**."

#### Step 6: Agree to Terms and Join Event

<p style="text-align: center;">
<img alt="Terms and Conditions" src="/images/aws_event/workshop_studio_tc.png">
</p>

- Review the *Terms and Conditions*, check the box, and click "**Join event**.
